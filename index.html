About This Project

Experience Reinvented is a temporary, non-commercial exploration currently in progress within the Adobe ecosystem. This site serves as a reference point during our time-limited research phase exploring Adobe technologies and AI integration possibilities.

Current Research Focus

We are not pursuing clients or commercial engagements during this exploratory period. Our current objective is exploratory research and partnership licensing for Adobe SDKs, with a particular emphasis on AEMaaCS and AI-driven workflows.

Important: This exploration does not intend to compete with any existing Adobe partners and is not actively seeking customers or offering commercial services at this time.

Exploration Areas

Adobe Experience Manager (AEM)

Investigating content management capabilities, headless architecture patterns, and integration opportunities with modern development workflows.

Adobe Site Optimizer

Exploring optimization strategies, performance enhancement techniques, and automated testing methodologies for digital experiences.

AI Agents & Autonomous Workflows

Researching the intersection of artificial intelligence and content management, focusing on automated decision-making and workflow optimization.

Generative AI Integrations

Examining how generative AI technologies can enhance content creation, personalization, and user experience within Adobe's ecosystem.

Adobe Partner SDK Access

As part of our current research exploration, we are working to establish appropriate access to Adobe SDKs through the research partnership track during this phase.

Current Focus: AEMaaCS

Our exploration currently emphasizes Adobe Experience Manager as a Cloud Service (AEMaaCS), examining its capabilities for modern content delivery, headless architecture implementation, and AI-enhanced content management workflows during this research period.

SDK access is being explored strictly for research, development, and partnership evaluation purposes during this exploratory phase, in alignment with Adobe's partner program guidelines.
